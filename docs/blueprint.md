# **App Name**: ShopFront

## Core Features:

- Business Showcase Creation: Allow businesses to create a showcase platform with their logo, name, tagline, and description.
- Product Management: Enable businesses to add, edit, and categorize products with details like title, description, price, category, and featured status. Also offer to show original and discounted price (optional).
- Search and Filter: Implement search and filter functionality for products, enabling users to find products by date and other relevant criteria.
- Contact Information: Display business contact information on the showcase platform.
- Admin Panel: Provide a secure admin panel for businesses to manage their showcase platform, including product data, and branding configurations. - Subscription Plans & Payment: Implement subscription plans (Basic: $50, Standard: $70, Premium: $100, Enterprise: $120 per month). Integrate payment options such as Mobile money, Mastercard, Visa card, and Google Pay.
- E-commerce Functionality: Integrate e-commerce features into the business' storefronts including add to cart, buy button and the ability to take payment and process orders

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant blue (#29ABE2) to convey trust and professionalism. 
- Background color: Light blue (#E5F6FD), a desaturated version of the primary color, creating a clean backdrop.
- Accent color: A dynamic purple (#9C27B0), creating visual interest in key elements such as buttons and links.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a versatile sans-serif font for clear and modern typography.
- Use simple, modern icons to represent product categories and features.
- Design product cards with a medium size (approximately 2 products fit on a 360px screen), similar to Amazon, to showcase products effectively.
- Use subtle animations and transitions to provide a smooth user experience.
